Hello! My name is Daeja Sutherland and I'm an illustrator, comic artist, and writer. I love exploring cultural, political, and social questions through fantasy illustration, and have a particular obsession with orcs. I mainly work with traditional media, specifically with graphite and pen. I color digitally or with watercolor and pencil crayon. After finishing my thesis comic, a story about two orc sisters, titled “The Back of the Orchard” I'm currently working on a graphic novel about a mother and son dealing with the aftermath of a failed experiment. Looking forward to connecting with you.
